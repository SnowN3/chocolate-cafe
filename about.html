The Everything Chocolate Cafe opened seven years ago by three self-professed chocolate lovers, who meet years ago while sampling chocolates at where else a Chocolate Fair. All three bakers have concocted delicious chocolates, chocolate cakes, chocolate drinks, chocolate...let's just say everything chocolate!! Their menu is extensive when it comes to delicious treats and drinks.

We have been named one of New York City's top ten cafes for five years in a row and counting. So if you're a true chocolate lover this is the place for you. Bring some friends and enjoy some of the best chocolate treats you ever tasted.
